THE NEXT ROUND


Client: Budweiser


Explore

Overview
What's a great way to focus on your friends rather than your phones while you enjoy a few beers together? The Next Round is a web app game where you lay your phones out on the bar and whoever touches it first buys the next round.

The Challenge
At my internship at AKQA, the client brief for Budweiser Holiday tasked us to come up with ideas that brought friends and family together over the holidays.

Given my extensive career behind a bar, I have invaluable insight when it comes to observing people interacting with each other, especially when they're with their friends that are home for the holidays. A game I've noticed people play is the "phone game," where everyone stacks their phones face down on the bar and whoever touches their phone first has to buy the next round.

Idea
I offered a digitized web application version of this game as a concept for Budweiser holiday, and the idea took hold. I was given the opportunity to have a hand in the entire creative concept production experience, from low- and high-fidelity wireframes, to the user flow walkthroughs, all the way to the user interface design. I also created some interactive prototypes using our wireframes for user testing.
