IrisCam

Client: LensCrafters


IrisCam was concepted as a way to get millenials to download an eye health app by LensCrafters - by giving them a high definition macro image of their own iris. However, when it was prototyped and tested on the VCU student body, it took on a life of its own.

The Veggie Butcher

Client: Hass Avocado Board


As an effort to encourage Americans to eat more fresh fruits and vegetables, this service prepares produce to order, so that shoppers can take it home and easily and quickly incorporate it into their meals.

Peak

Client: Mountain Hardwear


Outdoor enthusiasts are masters of planning; the true enthusiasts are always prepared for their adventures. However, no amount of planning can help when a crucial piece of gear is lost during activity — it can ruin a good trip and can potentially be dangerous. Peak is an RFID-based product innovation designed to prevent equipment loss so adventures stay amazing.

WuTang Forever

Client: The WuTang Clan


This campaign was to raise awareness surrounding the new album release, but instead of promoting the album itself, we instead chose to generate hype by crafting a massive interactive transmedia campaign all over New York City to teach the young people about the history of WuTang.

The Next Round

Client: Budweiser


What's a great way to focus on your friends rather than your phones while you enjoy a few beers together? The Next Round is a web app game where you lay your phones out on the bar and whoever touches it first buys the next round.

About Brittany


I was born in Florida, but lived most of my life in Richmond, Virginia.

After graduating from the Experience Design track at the VCU Brandcenter in May, 2015, I sold 90% of my belongings, converted a minivan into an RV and went on a 9 day cross-country road trip that ended in the Bay Area, where I currently reside.

I'm a creator and fixer by nature. I have an unquenchable thirst for knowledge. Almost 100% of everything I do is self-taught.

If I'm not working on a design or DIY project, you can most likely find me in the woods somewhere camping with my friends. I'm an avid outdoors enthusiast and adventurer, and find my inspiration in the middle of nowhere.

Fun Facts

I build vintage mopeds.

One of my favorite hobbies is building and renovating vintage mopeds. I have three currently: a 1979 Puch FreeSpirit, a 1980 Sachs General Five Star, and a 1977 Vespa Ciao.

I'm a diver.

I'm a lover of all things ocean, and received my PADI open water diving certification in 2012.

I'm a special effects makeup artist.

I taught myself how to do special effects makeup, and I now do commissioned work for characters for video shoots and other peoples' Halloween costumes. My zombie Little Mermaid costume won "Best Pop Culture Zombie" at the 2012 Richmond Zombie Walk.

I'm a veteran bartender.

I bartended for 10 years in all types of establishments ranging from fine dining restaurants to busy nightclubs.

I'm a diehard classic video gamer.

Much of my childhood was spent playing classic video games with my dad and brothers. My favorite game is the Legend of Zelda and have played -and beaten- every one that's been released. I even have a tattoo of the Hylian Crest from the main character's shield that represents courage, wisdom and strength.

I almost went to medical school.

When I got my Associate's degree, I applied and was accepted to VCU's School of Clinical Radiation Sciences to pursue radiology. My gut told me that the medical field wasn't for me, though, so I chose what made me happy instead - a creative field - and here I am!
