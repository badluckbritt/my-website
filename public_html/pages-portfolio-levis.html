Levi's Tablet Site Concept


Client: Levi's Vintage Clothing


Explore

Overview
One of my specialties is the ability to quickly create a simple interactive prototype to show how a concept can ultimately come to life.

The Challenge
The Levi's Vintage Clothing team had a concept for an interactive tablet site that they wanted to bring to life to pitch to the client.

The Idea
I created a few CSS animations and worked them into a simple parallaxing website using Adobe Muse. Later, they came to me to utilize my prototyping skills in Keynote to bring the overall concept to life for the pitch to the client. The kicker, though: the pitch was in less than 24 hours.

I was granted entry into the Levi's War Room at the agency, sat down with the art director, and worked all night to craft the interactive walkthrough in Keynote.

The client loved the idea the next morning, and later implemented the parallax elements, the scrubber and the interactive scrolling elements into their live website.
